Our Mission

TruthGuard is dedicated to combating misinformation in Philippine politics through advanced AI technology. We provide citizens, journalists, and researchers with reliable tools to verify news authenticity and make informed decisions.

Our hybrid machine learning and AI approach ensures accurate, real-time analysis of political content, helping preserve democratic discourse and informed public debate.

How TruthGuard Works

Our system combines multiple machine learning models with Gemini AI to provide comprehensive news analysis:

  • Machine Learning Models: Trained on Philippine political news datasets using Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Naive Bayes algorithms
  • Gemini AI Integration: Advanced natural language processing for content classification and factuality assessment
  • Cross-Verification: Real-time source checking against trusted Philippine news outlets
  • Weighted Scoring: Combines ML predictions with AI insights for accurate factuality scores
