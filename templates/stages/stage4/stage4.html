How to Play

You'll see a claim with contextual information and two reference snippets from reliable news sources. Read the claim carefully, analyze the evidence from both sources, and determine if the claim is Verified or False.

Scoring System

Verification Tips

  • Source Consistency: Do both sources align on key facts and timeline?
  • Official Confirmation: Look for government or institutional verification
  • Date Verification: Check if dates and timelines match the claim
  • Detail Accuracy: Verify specific numbers, names, and circumstances
  • Context Matters: Consider if partial truths create misleading impressions
