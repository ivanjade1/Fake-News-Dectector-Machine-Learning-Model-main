How to Play

You'll see 2 headlines per round. Drag the correct stamps to label each headline as REAL or FAKE.

Scoring System

Correct Answer: +25 XP
Speed Bonus (<5s): +5 XP
Lightning Bonus (<3s): +5 XP
Maximum per round: 35 XP
Using Hint: Max drops to 30 XP

What to Look For in Headlines

  • Fake Headlines: Sensational words (destroys, steals, betrays), absolute claims (all, never, forever), conspiracy terms (secretly, hidden), specific doomsday predictions
  • Real Headlines: Official government language (signs law, seeks, voids), neutral procedural terms, factual reporting without emotional manipulation
