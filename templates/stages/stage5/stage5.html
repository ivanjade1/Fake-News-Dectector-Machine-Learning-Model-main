How to Play

You'll analyze complete news articles to determine if they are Real or Fake. Read the article's URL, headline, source, date, and full content carefully. Use your critical thinking skills to identify signs of misinformation and make your verdict.

Scoring System

Analysis Tips

  • Source Credibility: Evaluate if the source is a reputable news organization
  • URL Authenticity: Check for suspicious domains or misspelled news sites
  • Content Quality: Look for poor grammar, sensational language, or extreme bias
  • Date Verification: Ensure publication dates align with content claims
  • Factual Consistency: Identify contradictions or implausible statements
  • Context Analysis: Consider if information is taken out of context
