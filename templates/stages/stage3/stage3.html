How to Play

You'll see one headline with two content previews. Choose the credible preview that reflects factual, neutral reporting. Optionally, highlight up to 3 red-flag phrases in the unreliable preview for bonus points.

Scoring System

Correct Answer: +30 XP
Speed Bonus (<8s): +10 XP
Lightning Bonus (<4s): +10 XP
Suspicious Phrases Bonus (1 phrase): +5 XP
Suspicious Phrases Bonus (2+ phrases): +15 XP
Maximum per round: 70 XP
Using Hint: Max drops to 60 XP

Red-Flag Phrases to Look For

  • Premature Finality: "ended permanently," "fully cleared," "final exoneration"
  • Unverifiable Secrecy: "midnight session," "little transparency," "quietly exempts"
  • Absolute Outcomes: "complete lockdown," "forced removal" without official orders
  • Uncited Claims: "no access to ruling," "did not cite," missing documentation
