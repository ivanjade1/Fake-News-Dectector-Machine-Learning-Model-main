How to Play

You'll see two different sources reporting on news. Choose the more trustworthy and reliable source based on URL accuracy, outlet credibility, headline quality, and article preview.

Scoring System

Correct Answer: +30 XP
Speed Bonus (<8s): +10 XP
Lightning Bonus (<4s): +10 XP
Maximum per round: 50 XP
Using Hint: Max drops to 40 XP

What to Look For in Sources

  • Reliable Sources: Established news outlets, correct domain spelling, proper TLD (.com, .net, .org), official government sites, recognized wire services
  • Unreliable Sources: Typosquatted domains, extra letters/subdomains, wrong TLDs (.co instead of .com), blog-style sites mimicking news, character swaps in domains
  • Red Flags: Double TLDs (.ph.co), misspelled domains, look-alike sites, unknown "newsy" domains, premature conclusions without official confirmation
